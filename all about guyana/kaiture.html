Kaiture Falls


Kaiture Falls is sure to sweep you off your feet, as it is one of the most powerful waterfalls in the world. It's fame is due to it's impressive combination of both height and volume.

To see the landmark Kaiture Falls of Guyana, Dagron Tours is the way to go! These falls offer a magical experience to anyone who sees it, as it is the largest single-drop waterfall in the world! Everyone who is in Guyana should take the oppertunity to see it, as the trip to the falls contains a wide variety of wildlife. After you take the trip and see the falls, you will realise it was worth it!

The Dagron Tours Kaiteur Falls excursion offers a thrilling and immersive experience into the heart of Guyana's natural beauty. The tour begins with a scenic flight over the lush rainforests, providing breathtaking aerial views of the dense jungle and winding rivers. Upon arrival at Kaiteur National Park, visitors embark on guided hikes through pristine wilderness, leading to various vantage points of the majestic Kaiteur Falls. Standing at 741 feet, Kaiteur Falls is one of the world's tallest single-drop waterfalls and is renowned for its powerful flow and stunning backdrop. The tour includes informative talks about the region's rich biodiversity, highlighting unique flora and fauna such as the rare golden frog and the endemic Guiana cock-of-the-rock bird. Dagron Tours ensures a comprehensive experience by also incorporating cultural elements, with guides sharing stories about the indigenous peoples and their connection to the land. The tour emphasizes eco-friendly practices, aiming to preserve the natural environment for future generations. Visitors are provided with ample time to soak in the spectacular views and capture memorable photographs. The return flight offers another chance to appreciate the untouched beauty of Guyana's landscape. Overall, the Dagron Tours Kaiteur Falls trip is an unforgettable adventure, showcasing one of South America's most remarkable natural wonders. Dagron Tours
